# HV Category Callouts

Portrait image cards linking to product category pages. Displays 2 columns on mobile, 4 on desktop. Each card has a background image, a gradient overlay, a tag label, a title, and a "Shop Now" button that appears on hover.

## Authoring

| Image | Tag | Title | Link |
|-------|-----|-------|------|
| Portrait product-category photo | Short label (e.g. "New Arrivals") | Category name | URL or linked text |

## Options

- **Image**: portrait-oriented (3:4) product or category photo
- **Tag**: optional neon-green badge above the title
- **Link**: the entire card is clickable; use a relative path (e.g. `/collections/safety-helmets`)

## Behavior

Cards animate in with a stagger as they enter the viewport. The "Shop Now" button fades in on hover.
