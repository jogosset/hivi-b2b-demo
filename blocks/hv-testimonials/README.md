# HV Testimonials

3-column customer review cards with star ratings, quote, avatar initials, and a "✓ Verified" badge. Initials are auto-generated from the reviewer's name.

## Authoring

**Header row (first row, 2 cells):**

| Eyebrow | Heading |
|---------|---------|
| 5-Star Reviews | What Our Customers Say |

**Review rows (one per card, 4 cells):**

| Name | Role · Company | Rating | Quote |
|------|---------------|--------|-------|
| Marcus Reynolds | Fleet Safety Manager · BuildCore Inc. | 5 | "We outfit 200+ field workers…" |

## Notes

- **Initials** are derived automatically from the name (first letter of each word, up to 2)
- **Rating**: enter a number 1–5; defaults to 5 if omitted
- Hover on a card slides an orange underline across the divider
- 1-column on mobile, 3-column on desktop (≥ 900 px)
